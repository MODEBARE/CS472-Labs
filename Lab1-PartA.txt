Block-level elements:
(They start on a new line and take up the full width available.)

<div>

<p>

<h1> to <h6>

<hr>

<section>

<header>

<footer>

<nav>

<aside>

<article>

<ol> / <li>

<ul> / <li>

<form>

<textarea>

Inline elements:
(They do not start on a new line and only take up as much width as necessary.)

<span>

<br>

<img>

<a>

<strong>

<i>

<input>

<button>

<select> / <option>

<label>
